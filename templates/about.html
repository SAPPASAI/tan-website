President's Message

Join the Movement – Be a Part of TAN!

Dear Friends and Well-Wishers,

It is with immense pride and deep responsibility that I serve as the President of Telugu Association of Nigeria (TAN). Our mission is to unite and uplift Telugu people in Nigeria through cultural preservation, community service, and mutual support.

TAN is not just an organization—it is a movement of the people, by the people, and for the people. We prioritize direct community impact and meaningful service initiatives that make a real difference.

Our Vision:
  • Seva (Service): Community service initiatives across Nigeria
  • Samskruthi (Culture): Celebrating our Telugu heritage
  • Samanathvam (Equality): Inclusive representation for all
  • Youth Empowerment: Developing future Telugu leaders

Join TAN today and be part of this important movement!

Jai Telugu! Jai TAN!

Policies & By-Laws

Article I: Name

The name and title of this organization shall be the Telugu Association of Nigeria (TAN). TAN shall be a not-for-profit organization registered in Nigeria.

Article II: Headquarters

The headquarters of TAN shall be located in Lagos, Nigeria. Administrative support personnel as approved by the Board of Directors shall staff the headquarters.

Article III: Objectives

TAN shall have the following objectives:

  1. To promote Telugu culture and heritage in Nigeria
  2. To assist community members through educational and social programs
  3. To organize cultural and social events
  4. To serve as a bridge between Telugu community and Nigerian society
Article IV: Membership

TAN offers several membership categories:

  • Life Member: One-time membership
  • Annual Member: Renewable yearly
  • Student Member: Discounted rate for students
